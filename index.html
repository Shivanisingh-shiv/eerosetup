What You Need for EERO Setup

Before beginning your EERO mesh WiFi setup, gather these essentials:

  • EERO router and any additional nodes (beacons)
  • Your EERO account login credentials
  • The EERO mobile app (iOS/Android)
  • An active modem with internet connection
  • Included Ethernet cable and power adapters
Tip: For optimal performance, place your primary EERO within 10 feet of your modem in an open area.

Step-by-Step EERO Setup

1

Download the EERO App

Install the EERO app from your device's app store. This is required for setup and management of your network.

2

Connect Primary EERO

Using the Ethernet cable, connect your main EERO to your modem. Plug in power and wait for the light to turn solid white.

3

Configure Through App

Follow the in-app instructions to create your network name (SSID) and password. The app will guide you through firmware updates.

4

Add Additional Nodes

Place additional EEROs about 40-50 feet apart. Use the app to add each node, following placement recommendations.

Troubleshooting EERO Setup

Common Issues and Solutions

  • Node Not Connecting: Move closer to another node during setup, then relocate after
  • Slow Speeds: Check modem connection and restart both modem and EERO
  • App Can't Find EERO: Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your mobile device
  • Intermittent Connection: Check for interference from other electronics or thick walls
  • Firmware Update Stuck: Wait at least 30 minutes before troubleshooting
Optimization Tip: Place EERO nodes in central locations at waist height, away from large metal objects, appliances, and electronics that may cause interference.
